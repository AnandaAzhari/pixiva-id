# MamQi Booth Project Context

## 1. Project Overview

MamQi Booth is a kiosk-first web photobooth experience for MamQi.

Version 1 supports Risol MamQi customer engagement: customers who purchase food can create and share a complimentary photo experience. The product is currently in the planning stage.

## 2. Vision

Create a simple, enjoyable photobooth experience that strengthens the MamQi brand today and can grow into a trusted commercial rental photobooth offering in the future.

## 3. Business Goals

- Increase customer engagement at Risol MamQi.
- Encourage customers to share their photos on social media.
- Improve MamQi brand visibility and recall.
- Build awareness of future MamQi Booth rental services.

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

Risol MamQi customers, including children and adults, who want a quick and enjoyable photo experience after making a purchase.

### Secondary Users

MamQi staff, who need the booth to be easy for customers to use with minimal assistance.

## 5. Current Scope (Version 1)

Version 1 is a single-event, Risol MamQi-branded experience with:

- Photo mode
- GIF mode
- Boomerang mode
- Camera preview
- Countdown
- Result preview
- QR download
- WhatsApp sharing
- Fullscreen kiosk mode

## 6. User Journey

1. A customer purchases Risol MamQi.
2. The customer opens MamQi Booth.
3. The customer selects Photo, GIF, or Boomerang mode.
4. The customer completes the countdown and captures media.
5. The customer previews the result.
6. The customer downloads or shares the result through a QR code or WhatsApp.
7. The session ends and the booth is ready for the next customer.

## 7. Supported Devices

### Primary Device

- iPad Mini 6 with Safari

### Secondary Support

- Chrome on desktop
- Edge on desktop

## 8. User Experience Goals

- Be understandable without instructions.
- Be touch friendly, with large and clear controls.
- Keep each customer flow short, ideally under one minute.
- Use minimal text and avoid unnecessary decisions.
- Feel fast, responsive, and suitable for children and adults.

## 9. Product Principles

- Prioritize a stable and enjoyable customer experience.
- Favor speed and clarity over visual decoration.
- Keep the experience focused on the customer journey.
- Use MamQi branding consistently.
- Make each session easy to begin, complete, and reset for the next user.

## 10. Out of Scope

The following are not part of Version 1:

- AI backgrounds
- Green screen
- Admin dashboard
- Multi-event support
- Multi-user management
- Online payment
- Analytics
- Cloud gallery

Version 1 is not intended to be a complete commercial photobooth platform.

## 11. Future Vision

Future product directions may include rental mode, event management, multi-event support, multi-client support, white-label branding, AI backgrounds, green screen, email sharing, and cloud galleries.

These directions inform the long-term product vision but are not commitments for Version 1.

## 12. Success Metrics

Version 1 success is indicated by:

- Customers completing the photo experience without staff assistance.
- Customers downloading or sharing their results.
- Positive customer engagement with the MamQi brand.
- Reliable use during a live Risol MamQi activation.
- Staff being able to operate and reset the booth confidently.
