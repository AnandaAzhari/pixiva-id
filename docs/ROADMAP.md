# MamQi Booth Roadmap

Version: 1.0.0

## Purpose

This roadmap communicates the intended product direction. It is a planning reference, not a commitment to implement every item.

## Version 1: Risol MamQi Experience

Status: Planning

Focus on a stable, kiosk-first experience for a single Risol MamQi activation:

- Camera preview and permission handling
- Photo capture
- Countdown
- GIF and Boomerang modes
- Result preview
- QR download and WhatsApp sharing
- Fullscreen kiosk experience

## Future Direction

Potential future product capabilities include:

- Rental mode
- Event management
- Admin dashboard
- Multi-event and multi-client support
- White-label branding
- AI background and green screen
- Email sharing and cloud gallery

These items are outside Version 1 and require separate planning, approval, and scope definition before implementation.
